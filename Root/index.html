Sats Accrual
When bridging Bitcoin to nBTC via the Nomic bridge, bridging fees are paid to $NOM stakers. The amount of sats you receive for staking $NOM is dependent on bridging fees and your percentage of total $NOM staked. The bridge is in an experimental phase with high fees and current rate of accrual is not a reliable indicator of future rewards.
APR
There is no reliable data yet available to provide precise staking APR. Default APR value will be updated periodically until such data becomes available. Nomic inflationary rewards are subject to annual 'thirdening', whereby staking rewards will decrease by 1/3 on 1st February annually. By default the 'Thirdening' checkbox is checked to account for the thirdening as an average APR over the number of years selected.
